In 2015 I had a project on videregående (equivalent to high school) where I chose to redesign the GUI of alistairmaclean.com. The reason behind this, was that I had previously seen and been dissapointed by it. MacLean almost exclusively books with action and suspense, and I didn’t think this site resembled that the slightest.

Tools

I built the site using HTML and CSS and a little bit JavaScript. I made the logo in Illustrator and the header image in Photoshop.
